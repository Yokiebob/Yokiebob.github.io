m to switch to level making mode (fly)

q to change planet radius size

f to change planet color ([r,b,g,a], 0-255, [255,255,0,0] is defined as kill, all zeros is normal green blocks)

h to place planet

g to place moving planet, starting position and ending position

o to change moving planet speed, scales with distance

e to place red box

r to remove last placed red box

p to remove last placed planet (moving and normal)

j to print all level data

Left and Right arrow keys to change levels



first planet is light source, I suggest making it white and far away, but don't lose track of player starting point
